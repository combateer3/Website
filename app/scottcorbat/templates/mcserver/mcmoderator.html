Moderator

Moderators are mid-level staff to handle common issues and disputes.

Command Syntax Description
back /back Teleports you to your location prior to tp/warp.
gc /gc [all] Reports memory, uptime and tick info.
getpos /getpos [player] Get current position or that of another player.
hat /hat [remove] Equip held item as hat.
heal /heal [player] Heals yourself or another player.
invsee /invsee <player> See the inventory of other players.
kick /kick <player> [reason] Kicks a specified player with a reason.
mute /mute <player> [datediff] Mutes or unmutes another player.
tempban /tempban <player> <datediff> <reason> Temporarily ban a player.
tpa /tpa <player> Request to teleport to specified player.
tpaaccept /tpaccept [otherplayer] Accepts a teleport request.
tpacancel /tpacancel [player] Cancel all outstanding tp requests, or cancel with a specific player.
tpdeny /tpdeny Reject a teleport request.
