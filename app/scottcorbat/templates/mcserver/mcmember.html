Member

The default role given to all new players who join the server.

Command Syntax Description
afk /afk Marks yourself as afk.
balance /balance <player> Get balance of a player.
depth /depth Get current depth.
helpop /helpop <message> Send message to the online admins.
ignore /ignore <player> Ignore or unignore another player.
info /info Gives general information about the server.
seen /seen <player> Shows last logout time of a player.
suicide /suicide Causes you to perish.
pay /pay <player> <amount> Pays another player from your balance.
paytoggle /paytoggle Toggles whether you are accepting payments.
help /help [page number] Displays help info about commands.
r /r <message> Quick reply to the last player who messaged you.
msg /msg <player> <message> Send a private message to another player.
rules /rules Displays the rules of the server.
worth /worth <<itemname>|<id>|hand|inventory|blocks> [-][amount] Calculates the worth of items in hand or as specified.
